## Purpose

The Technical Steering Committee (TSC) is responsible for the technical direction, quality, and coherence of the WEKAOU® standard.

---

## Responsibilities

### 1. Specification Governance
- **Approve RFCs**: Review and vote on Standard Change Proposals
- **Manage Releases**: Decide when to cut new versions of the specification
- **Enforce Quality**: Ensure changes meet technical rigor standards
- **Resolve Conflicts**: Arbitrate technical disputes

### 2. Ecosystem Stewardship
- **Certify Implementations**: Approve compliance via `compliance-suite`
- **Establish Working Groups**: Create and charter domain-specific groups
- **Guide Tooling**: Oversee `compliance-suite` and `kos-community-edition` development
- **Maintain Documentation**: Ensure clarity and accessibility

### 3. Community Engagement
- **Monthly Public Meetings**: Open attendance, public agenda
- **Quarterly Roadmap Updates**: Publish priorities and progress
- **Annual Technical Report**: State of the ecosystem review

---

## Composition

### Size
5-9 members (start with 5, scale as ecosystem grows)

### Eligibility
- Active ACA member for at least 6 months
- Demonstrated technical expertise in:
  - Ontologies, knowledge representation, OR
  - Systems architecture, OR
  - Governance systems, OR
  - Adjacent domains relevant to WEKAOU
- At least 5 significant contributions to WEKAOU repos

### Selection
Elected annually by ACA membership (see `aca/statutes.md` for process)

### Term
1 year, renewable up to 3 consecutive terms

---

## Decision-Making

### Quorum
60% of TSC members must participate in votes

### Voting Methods
- **Consensus**: Preferred (unanimous or near-unanimous agreement)
- **Simple Majority**: Fallback for contentious issues
- **Supermajority** (2/3): For major decisions (e.g., chartering Working Groups, major spec changes)

### Veto Power
None. No single TSC member can block a decision.

### Conflict of Interest
Members must recuse themselves from votes where they have direct financial interest.

---

## Meetings

### Regular Meetings
**Frequency**: Monthly (first Thursday of each month)
**Duration**: 90 minutes
**Format**: Video call (recorded and published)
**Agenda**: Published 3 days prior, community can propose items

### Emergency Meetings
Called by any TSC member for urgent issues (security vulnerabilities, critical bugs).

### Meeting Notes
Published in `tsc/meetings/YYYY-MM.md` within 48 hours.

## Powers and Limits

### What TSC CAN Do
✅ Approve or reject RFCs
✅ Merge PRs to `wekaou-specification`
✅ Establish Working Groups
✅ Certify implementations
✅ Interpret ambiguous spec language

### What TSC CANNOT Do
❌ Amend the Charter (requires Steering Committee + membership vote)
❌ Change license of specification (requires Steering Committee approval)
❌ Expel members (handled by Code of Conduct enforcement process)
❌ Make unilateral financial commitments (requires Steering Committee)

---

## Accountability

### Transparency
- All TSC votes are public (anonymized ballots, but results published)
- Meeting recordings published (can redact sensitive security discussions)
- RFC decisions include rationale

### Recall Process
If 1/3 of ACA members petition for a TSC member's removal:
1. Formal hearing (member can defend)
2. Membership votes (2/3 majority required to remove)
3. Replacement elected in emergency election
