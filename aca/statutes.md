## Article II: Membership

### 2.1 Membership Categories

#### Individual Members
**Eligibility**: Any person who has:
- Made at least 3 accepted contributions to WEKAOU repositories, OR
- Participated in at least 5 RFC discussions, OR
- Been nominated by existing member and approved by TSC

**Rights**:
- Vote in TSC elections
- Propose RFCs
- Join Working Groups
- Access to member-only communication channels

**Obligations**:
- Adhere to Code of Conduct
- Sign CLA for contributions
- Participate in at least 2 governance events per year (votes, meetings, etc.)

#### Organizational Members
**Eligibility**: Organizations that:
- Implement or use WEKAOU in production, AND
- Contribute financially or in-kind to ACA operations

**Rights**:
- All Individual Member rights
- Nominate 1 representative to vote
- Sponsor Working Groups
- Display "ACA Member" badge

**Tiers**:
- **Platinum** ($50k+/year): 2 TSC nomination slots
- **Gold** ($25k+/year): 1 TSC nomination slot
- **Silver** ($10k+/year): Priority support
- **Bronze** ($5k+/year): Recognition on website

### 2.2 Membership Lifecycle

**Application**: Submit via governance repository issue
**Approval**: TSC reviews quarterly
**Renewal**: Annual (automatic if participation criteria met)
**Termination**: 
- Voluntary withdrawal
- Failure to meet participation criteria (2 year inactivity)
- Code of Conduct violation (per enforcement process)

## Article III: Elections

### 3.1 TSC Elections

**Schedule**: Annual, every November
**Eligibility to Vote**: All Active Members (contributed within last 12 months)
**Eligibility to Stand**: Members with at least 6 months membership

**Process**:
1. Call for nominations (2 weeks)
2. Candidate statements published
3. Voting period (1 week) via Condorcet method
4. Results announced publicly
5. New TSC takes office January 1

**Terms**: 
- 1 year
- Maximum 3 consecutive terms
- Staggered: 50% of seats each year

### 3.2 Steering Committee Elections

**Schedule**: Biennial, every January
**Eligibility**: TSC nominates candidates, membership votes
**Terms**: 2 years, staggered

## Article IV: Finances

### 4.1 Revenue Sources
- Organizational membership dues
- Certification fees (compliance-suite)
- Grants and donations
- Conference sponsorships

### 4.2 Expenditures
- Infrastructure (hosting, domains, tools)
- Events (conferences, meetups)
- Legal and accounting
- Contributor stipends (for significant work)

### 4.3 Budget Approval
- Annual budget proposed by Steering Committee
- Requires 2/3 approval from TSC
- Published publicly in `aca/finances/` (anonymized if needed)

---

## Article V: Amendments

These statutes may be amended by:
1. Proposal via RFC (label: `governance-amendment`)
2. TSC review and recommendation
3. Steering Committee approval (2/3 majority)
4. Member ratification (simple majority)
