# Alliance for Coherent AI (ACA)

> **Stewards of the WEKAOU Standard**

---

## About the ACA

The Alliance for Coherent AI (ACA) is the governing body responsible for stewarding the WEKAOU® open standard for Cognitive Governance Systems.

**Mission**: To ensure the standard evolves transparently, equitably, and in service of the broader community through evidence-based, participatory governance.

## Membership

The ACA welcomes both individual and organizational members who contribute to the WEKAOU ecosystem.

**Individual Members**: Contributors, developers, researchers  
**Organizational Members**: Companies, institutions, foundations (4 tiers: Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze)

See [statutes.md](./statutes.md) for eligibility criteria and [members.md](./members.md) for the application process.

## Governance Structure

```
ACA Membership
    ↓ (elects)
Steering Committee ← Administrative & Strategic
    ↓ (charters)
Technical Steering Committee (TSC) ← Technical Direction
    ↓ (establishes)
Working Groups ← Domain Expertise
```

See [../tsc/mandate.md](../tsc/mandate.md) for TSC details.
